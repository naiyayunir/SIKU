Delivering Quality Healthcare Solutions for All

Incorporated in 2021, Griya Bina Sejahtera operates in the healthcare sector, specializing in the sales of medical devices and the provision of healthcare clinic management services. The company is poised to serve as a distribution partner for health equipment sourced from international manufacturers. Our product portfolio encompasses a variety of brands, with a particular focus on the procurement of medical devices, specifically in the fields of Surgery, Obstetrics and Gynecology (Obgyn), and Anesthesia.

Our Services

The company provides exceptional services to customers throughout Indonesia, tailored to meet the unique needs of individuals, families, and businesses.
